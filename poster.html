WHAT IS UTHM BARGAIN?

UTHM Bargain is a dedicated marketplace platform exclusively for the Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia community. It connects among students in a secure trading environment where members can buy and sell items within the campus community.

WHY USE UTHM BARGAIN?

  • Campus-Exclusive: Trade only with verified UTHM community members
  • Student Budget-Friendly: Find affordable academic materials, electronics, and more
  • Convenient Transactions: Easy campus meet-ups for item exchanges
  • Verified Profiles: Enhanced security with UTHM email verification
  • Matric Number System: Each user has a unique ID (e.g., AD24XXXX) tied to their UTHM account

FEATURES

  • Smart Search: Find items with powerful filtering options
  • Item Listings: View price, condition (new/used), and availability status
  • Seller Profiles: See faculty information and contact details
  • Item Categories: Browse by Academic, Electronics, Fashion, Room & Furniture
  • Featured Items: Discover highlighted products on the homepage
  • My Listings: Manage your own product listings easily
  • Faculty Verification: Shows which faculty the seller belongs to (e.g., FSKTM)
  • Free Listings: No cost to post items for sale
